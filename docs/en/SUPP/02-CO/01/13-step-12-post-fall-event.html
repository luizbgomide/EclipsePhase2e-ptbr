Step 12: Post-Fall Event

Now you get to see what major event influenced your life after the Fall. Roll on Table 12 and apply results, then proceed to Step 13.

THE STORY:

This step deals with more recent changes to your character’s life. Did you run across some hazard that survived the Fall? Were you recruited for a gatecrashing mission? Did you switch faction allegiances? Maybe you picked up a hobby or a an odd skill on the side? What sparked or motivated these changes?

12.0 Post-Fall Event [d100]

d100Result
00Gain +1 ego Flex and roll on the Story Event table.
01–02You win the gatecrashing lottery and a free ticket to Pandora. Roll on the Gatecrashing Event table.
03You are embroiled in underworld affairs. Roll on the Crime Event table.
04You find a morph that fits you like a glove. Gain the Morph Attunement trait. Roll on the Random Morph tables.
05You are recruited into Firewall early after the Fall. Roll on the Firewall Event table.
06You attempt to modify your mind, but it goes poorly. Gain the Messy Mind trait.
07You fall victim to a terrorist attack or factional dispute. Roll on the Random Morph tables.
08You reinvent yourself. Increase one aptitude by 5 and reduce another by 5. Roll 1d6: 1 COG, 2 INT, 3 REF, 4 SAV, 5 SOM, 6 WIL.
09You get caught in a post-Fall breakout and are infected with Watts-MacLeod (EP2). Gain the Psi (Level 1) trait and 2 psichi sleights (pick or roll randomly on the Sleights table).
10You find it hard to cope with the Fall and its aftermath. Gain the Addiction (Level 2) trait (EP2).
11–12You get caught in a post-Fall breakout and are infected with Watts-MacLeod (EP2). +10 Psi skill and gain the Psi (Level 2) trait and 1 psi-gamma sleight (pick or roll randomly on the Sleights table).
13–14You use the post-Fall chaos to establish a new identity. Gain a fake ego ID (EP2) for free.
15–16You piss off some influential people and are made into an example. −10 @-rep, g-rep, or c-rep.
17After surviving the Fall, you decide some self-defense training is in order. +10 to either Guns or Melee skill.
18–19You run afoul of a criminal cartel agent. You walk away unscathed, but the matter is far from resolved. Gain the Enemy trait (EP2).
20–21You commit a crime, get caught, and suffer the punishment. Gain the Restricted Behavior (Level 2) trait (EP2).
22You have died enough times that your mind really can’t take it any more. Roll on the Random Morph tables and gain the Mental Disorder (Phobia: Death) trait (also on Disorders, EP2).
23You interact with people from an opposing faction often, and you get good at pressing their buttons. +10 Provoke skill.
24An ownerless bot follows you and never leaves your side. Gain a servitor, saucer, or gnat bot (EP2) for free.
25An unknown party leaves you a portable QE comm unit with a qubit reservoir (EP2), telling you only to expect a call in the future.
26You travel extensively. Gain a new language and roll on the Random Morph tables.
27You’ve gotten really good at resleeving. Roll on the Random Morph tables and gain the Adaptability (Level 2) trait (EP2).
28You create a major diplomatic incident. Gain the Black Mark (Level 2) trait (EP2). Roll on the Faction table to determine which.
29You barely survive a murder attempt. Increase INT by 5.
30A close-call with people out to get you puts you on the defensive. Gain the Data Control (Level 2) trait.
31You come into possession of 5 doses of the psi-opener drug (EP2). Roll on the Sleights table to determine effects.
32You fall on hard times. Lose one level of Resources trait. If you don’t have Resources, roll on the Random Morph tables but only take a synthmorph or infomorph of MP 2 or less.
33You establish a relationship with someone in-the-know. Gain the Contact trait (EP2). Choose a rep network or roll randomly on the Rep Networks table.
34You are victimized by an async. Gain the Psi Defense (Level 2) trait (EP2).
35A long string of personal failures has you questioning your own resolve. Reduce WIL by 5.
36No matter how often your friends warn you, you are promiscuous about your online data. Gain the Data Footprint (Level 2) trait.
37Nothing ever seems to go your way — your cursed luck is legendary. Gain the Bad Luck trait (EP2).
38You complete a project of major importance. +10 to the rep score most appropriate to your faction.
39A project of major importance fails under your direction. −10 to the rep score most appropriate to your faction.
40You are obsessed with the Factors. +10 Know: Factors skill.
41You try a lot of morphs, but only one hits the spot. Gain the Morph Familiarity trait (EP2). Choose a morph or roll randomly on the Random Morph tables.
42An unpopular choice burns many bridges. Gain the Blocklisted Rep trait (EP2). Choose a rep network or roll randomly on the Rep Networks table.
43A friend or relative opts for true death, but bequeaths you their estate. Gain Resources (Level 2) trait.
44You experiment with a number of morphs. Increase SOM by 5 and roll on the Random Morph tables.
45You become complacent in a bout of post-Fall depression. Reduce SOM by 5.
46As transhumanity re-organizes, you find a role in influencing others. Increase SAV by 5.
47You have difficulty coming to grips with regular resleeving. Gain the Identity Crisis trait (EP2) and roll on the Random Morph tables.
48A resleeve goes particularly poorly, and now it haunts you. Roll on the Random Morph tables and gain the Morphing Disorder (Level 2) trait (EP2).
49You are an avid fan of gatecrashing XP and vids. +10 Know: Exoplanets skill.
50You take up a sport. +10 Athletics skill.
51You find peace on one specific habitat of your choice. Gain the Habitat Tranquility trait.
52You have an accident with a drone, so you get really good at making them obey you. Gain the Drone Whisperer trait.
53You decide to take a risk and let the universe decide your fate. Gain the No Backup Insurance trait (EP2).
54Someone impersonates you. Gain the Stolen Identity trait.
55You switch factional allegiances. Roll for your new faction. Gain Know: [New Faction] Interests 10 skill.
56You spend a significant portion of your life in one habitat. Gain the Home Turf trait.
57You are hired to work on a ship and eventually become the captain. Gain the Spacecraft trait.
58You do the right thing but piss off someone with power. −10 f-rep or c-rep.
59You make friends with a group of AGIs online. Gain the AGI Affinity trait.
60You rely heavily on forks. Gain the Improved Beta trait.
61You are caught in an isolated outpost with few resources, but you make do. Gain the Scrounging Talent trait.
62You push the boundaries of using and re-merging forks. Gain the Merging Mastery trait.
63Unknown to you, someone takes an unfriendly interest in your affairs. Gain the Subverted Mind trait.
64You spend years on a micrograv habitat. +10 Free Fall skill.
65The merging of an overdue fork goes poorly, and inaccessible backups leave you permanently changed. Gain the Botched Merge trait.
66You are embroiled in a messy professional dispute. −10 r-rep or c-rep.
67You lose a contractual dispute in Extropian space. Gain the Deferred Indenture trait.
68You have an exceptionally horrible resleeving experience and swear off that type of morph. Roll on the Random Morph tables and gain the Morph Aversion (Level 2) trait.
69The state of post-Fall transhumanity inspires you to up your game. Increase COG by 5.
70Through a strange set of circumstances, you end up with a stash of 5 inquisitive green petal flowers (EP2).
71You get stuck with a boring, repetitive job, but at least you get really good at it. Gain one specialization (EP2) in a skill of your choice.
72Doing your part to aid transhumanity’s regrowth, you have a kid. Gain the Dependent trait.
73You are killed in a severe accident. Decrease REF by 5 and roll on the Random Morph tables.
74You achieve something that the members of your faction will never forget. Gain the Gold Star (Level 2) trait.
75You impressively win a public competition. Increase REF by 5.
76A troll hacker damages your rep but leaves you with a gift. −10 to a rep score of your choice but gain a kaos ALI (EP2).
77You take in an abandoned animal. Gain a smart dog, smart monkey, or smart rat (EP2).
78You discover that you work well with machines. Gain the Drone Affinity trait (EP2).
79Someone you trust takes advantage of you. Reduce INT by 5.
80A severe personal failure inspires you to make some radical changes. Increase WIL by 5.
81You step up for your faction in a way few people will forget. Gain the Untarnished Rep trait.
82You team up with a partner to get the job done. Gain the Minion/Partner trait.
83You pick up drone piloting as a hobby. +10 Pilot skill in a field of your choice.
84Your exceptional nature is noticed. Gain the Patron trait (EP2).
85You win a bodyware lottery. Start play with an extra ware of Major complexity (your choice) installed in your morph, but you are followed by a company bot that records everything you do for promotional and evaluation purposes.
86–87You experience a catastrophic sleeving accident. Start play with 10 points of stress and roll on the Random Morph tables.
88You fall for someone’s smooth lies. Reduce SAV by 5.
89You split off an alpha fork to handle an important situation, but it does not come back. Gain the Errant Fork trait.
90–91You fall in with a new crowd — one that will have your back. Gain the Allies trait (EP2).
92You are inadvertently featured in a prominent meme. Gain the Meme Fame trait.
93With so many friends changing morphs, you get good at telling who’s who. +10 Kinesics skill.
94Your best friend is an uplift. Gain the Uplift Affinity trait.
95You make a dumb and costly mistake. Decrease COG by 5.
96While no specific morph outshines the others, you definitely lean towards a type. Roll on the Random Morph tables and gain the Morph Mastery trait.
97You spend a long time living or interacting with synthmorphs or infomorphs. Gain the Synth Kinesics trait.
98You face trouble when one of your forks refuses to come back. Gain the Divergent Personality trait.
99You encounter something you hope never to experience again. Gain the Stress Vulnerability trait.
